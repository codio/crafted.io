who are we?

Why work with Crafted?

It's simple - when you choose to work with us, you're working with a team of dedicated professionals who are willing to do whatever it takes to get the job done. We've built apps that are in use by hundreds of thousands of users world-wide, including Coca-Cola, NetVentures, Mobile Energy Apps, Codio, ApplicationCraft and many more.

Our customer-centric approach is at at the heart of what we do. We won't say we can do what we can't do. Our prices are very reasonable. We are very good at delivering on-time and on-budget. We don't try to tie you in or tie you up. We develop using open and standard technologies. We communicate exceptionally well.

Simply put, we love what we do and we love doing it well. So, read on to find out more about more about us or get in touch with us
